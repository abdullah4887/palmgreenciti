COMPANY OVERVIEW

Palm Green Citi Ltd. with RC Number: 1080563 is a fast-growing indigenous pan-African multi-business company situated in Nigeria and established in the year 2012.

Over the years, our team has passed on their expertise and passion for trading to all of the teams that work in 15 different states in Nigeria and built a strong trading organization together. The management at Palm Green Citi Ltd. holds extensive knowledge and experience in the fields of agriculture (commodity trading, and farming), mining, and exportation, marine services, building construction and engineering, logistics, commodity merchant, project and support management, and consultancy services.

Palm Green Citi trades in a diversified portfolio of products that are classified as, Agro & Consumer Goods, Sulphur and fertilizers, Minerals Resources, and Others with rapid expansion stems from the efficient, experienced, and professional members of the organization, whose key focus is to create sustainable value for all stakeholders.

The culture in Palm Green Citi places strong emphasis on fulfilling the needs of customers, partners, and suppliers, and at the same time fostering long-term relationships with renowned international partners in Asia, the United States of America, Europe, and South Africa.

At the core of the overall company strategy is to stay customer-oriented and continue to diversify in trade & and geographies to become a global conglomerate. The company’s generic evolution has been an inspiration and encouragement for the entire organization, and by upholding its core values strongly and capitalizing on its strengths, Palm Green Citi will continue to pursue its vision and mission.

CORE VALUES
At Palm Green Citi Ltd., we uphold 3 core values that guide everything we do. It unites all employees connecting us across several business units and the vast geography. These values are integral to the way we work and the decisions we make.

Commitment
Palm Green Citi’s team lives by the principle of being committed to our clients in all our trade activities. At the same time, we also value the dedication and hard work displayed by our team while we reward them to encourage them further.

Creating value
Creating value for our clients is very important to Us. We are dedicated to providing goods and services of excellent quality with competitive prices to our entire client base.

Fostering relationships
We are a customer-oriented company and believe in developing strong, positive, and long-lasting relationships with all our partners. Our partner’s success is just as important as ours and grounded in every individual’s vision to attain great heights.
Our Strength
Our strengths give us a competitive edge and set us apart from all our competitors. The combination of all our strengths below allows us to provide maximum satisfaction to our clients and enables us to be their preferred partner.

Passion
The foundation of Palm Green Citi is the zeal and passion shared by the entire organization, most especially its BOD. It is our passion to excel and maintain the highest levels of efficiency in the challenging nature of our businesses, which drives us to achieve greater heights in the industry.

Experience
Palm Green Citi's management team holds extensive knowledge and experience in its business field and can make the best decisions and swiftly troubleshoot any problems that might arise. Over the years, they have led by example and continue to educate and share their skills with the entire team.

Competitiveness
Palm Green Citi's forte lies in trading and marketing at the best market prices. We achieve this by leveraging on our presence in the world’s developing markets. Our competitive nature drives us to be on a constant lookout for products that will be a good bet for the growth of the company in the long term.

Geographical presence
In a short period,Palm Green Citi has sprawled its offices and teams across the country. We pride ourselves in being able to remain in close liaison with our clients due to our good relationship management. It allows us to communicate effectively with the client base and efficiently execute all transactions or troubleshoot any problems that may arise.

Risk management abilities
Our ability to identify the various risks involved in our trade well beforehand has proven to be an important aspect in ensuring smooth business. Palm Green Citi has extremely disciplined risk management practices which is a unique capability that differentiates us from the rest.

Efficiency in execution
Efficiency at Palm Green Citi is implemented from the inception (farm) of the inspection of goods to the time that our goods reach the warehouses of our buyers. This allows us to economically allocate our resources and create happy experiences for our clients.
