OUR TEAM

The foundation upon which our team is created is based upon the premise that motivated people and long-standing relationships are the ultimate tools of success and creativity, energy, perseverance, and loyalty are just as important as a platinum resume. We have a team of highly qualified and experienced professionals with proven problem-solving, consulting, and analytical skills. Our Team consists of business-orientated Leadership, associates, experts & and technical specialists to ensure sustainability, attain success, and provide world-class services to our clients.
OUR LEADERSHIP
MR. MUSTAPHA BABA ADAMU
CO-FOUNDER & MANAGING DIRECTOR

Mr. Mustapha Baba Adamu is an entrepreneur, Co-founder, and Managing Director at Palm Green Citi Ltd. As Co-Founder he has played leadership roles in Palm Green Citi since its inception in 2012 and has over 10 years of entrepreneurial and professional experience in Agribusiness, exporting, Agro/Industrial raw materials production, and supply chain locally and internationally. He is a competent and polished strategist and executor in public and private service needs with over 20 years of combined experience in capacity building, executive support, business development, and project management. He has constantly been seeking and leveraging his wide-ranging service skills in service-related positions and business opportunities.

Mr. Mustapha Adamu has planned, designed, and developed operations of women and youth empowerment program in the Bauchi State Grassroot Association of Nigeria; Office of the First Lady as coordinator resulting in a 38% uplift in efficiency. He worked as a Leadership, Management, and Sustainable consultant with the USAID-MSH programme in Kaduna, Kwara, and Kogi respectively in 2009. Currently, He is a Consultant Partner with Kaduna State Enterprise Development Agency, where he heads Operations and facilitates capacity building and empowerment for MSMEs (World Bank Supported). Since 2015, he worked as the Head of Administration and Operations for Frutas & Weinberg Limited, a company engaged in maritime and salvage operations for the National Inland Waterways Authority under PPP, where he planned workforce usage, equipment requirements, and office/ field structure to optimize workflows which led to 40% increased workflow.



Mr. Mustapha Adamu spearheaded all businesses as the Managing Director at Palm Green Citi he has transformed Palm Green Citi’s trading and export line from a small base to be among the top 5 players in the industry in just over 10 years. After co-founding Palm Green Citi Mr. Mustapha held a senior leadership position as a Managing Partner at M.M Kuptima Nigeria Limited, a construction company and marine litter and water hyacinth project executor for the Nigerian Maritime Administration & Safety Agency and this earned him a board member of the AIMS Shipping and Marine Service Consortium.

Mr. Mustapha Adamu holds a Bachelor of Technology degree (Hons) in Urban and Regional Planning from the prestigious Abubakar Tafawa Balewa University, Bauchi State, Nigeria, Advance Project Monitoring and Evaluation (Certified) amongst others.


AMB. SAMSONDEEN O. OLAGUNJU
EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR, BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY & OPERATIONS

AMB. Samsondeen O. Olagunju came on board to impact positively and drive the train of business to a great peak; he has been playing a crucial leadership role at Palm Green Citi since he joined in 2023 with a constant effort to rebrand and restructure the operations of the company towards achieving tremendous success with credibility to enhancing business for sustainability. He has over 5 years of entrepreneurial and professional experience in Business Development and Management, Agribusiness Logistics, Exportation, Agro/Industrial raw materials production, and processing, Farming, and Aquaculture, Branding, Marketing, and Networking across Africa and the Diaspora. He is a competent and polished creative thinker, strategist, and risk-taker with experience in the business sector. He has a quiet number of historic partnerships with international partners and investors in Europe, the United Kingdom, Asia, Africa, the United Arab Emirates, and America, while leveraging on his wide-ranging international network, and services to humanity in service-related positions and business opportunities to promote the growth of the company.



AMB. Samsondeen O. Olagunju spearheaded all business operations at Palm Green Citi while restructuring and promoting the company’s image to turn a golden name in the industry. He has been in business and working with various NGOs locally and internationally since 2010, he has been a member Board of Directors in two prominent companies, a consultant and a partner to Harvest Group LLC in Riga Latvia, and manages business across Africa, and owns an establishment.
